Find Safe – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Find Safe (“Find Safe”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). Find Safe is a marketplace platform that enables users to buy and sell second-hand items and, in the future, goods offered by stores, with a strong focus on safety and fraud prevention. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

By accessing or using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Services.

2. Scope and Controller

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Find Safe Services worldwide. For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, Find Safe is the controller of your personal information, unless otherwise stated.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide to Us

We may collect the following information directly from you when you register, create a profile, list or purchase items, or communicate with us:

  • Account Information: Name, username, email address, phone number, password, profile photo.
  • Contact Details: Billing address, shipping address, pickup address and other contact details.
  • Listings and Transactions: Item descriptions, photos, price, category, condition, and related details of items you buy or sell.
  • Communications: Messages you send through in-app chat, inquiries to other users, and communication with our support team.
  • Feedback and Reviews: Ratings, reviews, complaints, dispute information, and other feedback you submit.

3.2 Government ID and Address Verification Data

To provide a safer, scam-resistant marketplace and to verify user identities, we may ask certain users (for example, high-risk accounts, frequent sellers, or when required for dispute resolution) to submit:

  • Government-issued identity documents (such as national ID, tax ID, driving license, passport, or similar ID),
  • Address proof documents (such as utility bills or official documents showing your name and address),
  • Selfie photographs or short verification videos to match your identity with submitted documents.

This information is collected solely for identity verification, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, and to comply with applicable legal obligations. It is never displayed publicly to other users.

3.3 Payment and Financial Information

When you make or receive payments through the Services, payment processing is performed by third-party payment processors or banking partners. Depending on the method used, we may receive limited information such as transaction IDs, payment status, and partial card or account details. We do not store full payment card numbers on our own servers.

3.4 Location Information

With your permission, we may collect approximate or precise location information from your device to:

  • Show nearby listings and sellers,
  • Facilitate pickup and delivery,
  • Improve fraud detection and security.

You can control location permissions through your device settings. Disabling location may limit certain features.

3.5 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device and Technical Data: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, app version, language, and time zone.
  • Usage Data: Pages or screens viewed, features used, search queries, items viewed or saved, time and date of visits, clickstream data.
  • Log and Diagnostic Data: Crash reports, performance data, and diagnostic logs to help us improve stability and user experience.

3.6 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies to recognize your device, remember your preferences, analyze usage, and personalize content. You may be able to control cookies through your browser or device settings; however, disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Services.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account and user profile.
  • To enable you to list, discover, buy, and sell items through the marketplace.
  • To facilitate communication between buyers, sellers, stores, couriers, and our support team.
  • To process payments, commissions, and refunds, and to keep records of transactions.
  • To verify identity and address, prevent fraud, detect suspicious or prohibited activity, and protect users and the platform.
  • To operate, maintain, improve, and personalize the Services.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests or inquiries.
  • To send you service-related notifications, such as changes to orders, disputes, policies, or security alerts.
  • To perform analytics, reporting, and research to improve safety, performance, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, tax, and accounting obligations.
  • To enforce our Terms of Use and other agreements.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (Where Applicable)

In some regions, such as the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and other jurisdictions with similar laws, we process personal information under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Contract: To provide the Services and perform our contract with you.
  • Legitimate Interests: To secure the platform, prevent fraud, improve services, and support customer service, in a way that does not override your rights and interests.
  • Consent: Where required (for example, for optional marketing communications or access to precise location), we rely on your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with laws, regulations, court orders, or lawful requests from authorities.

6. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

6.1 With Other Users

When you use the Services, certain profile information and listing information may be visible to other users, such as your username, profile photo, approximate location (e.g., city), ratings, and items you have listed for sale. We do not publicly display your government ID or full address.

6.2 With Delivery and Logistics Partners

To arrange pickup, inspection, and delivery of items, we may share limited information with trusted delivery and logistics partners, such as:

  • Name and contact number of sender and recipient,
  • Pickup and delivery addresses,
  • Order ID and basic product details.

Government ID images or numbers are not shared with delivery partners unless strictly required by law or safety regulations.

6.3 With Service Providers

We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, identity verification, cloud hosting, analytics, marketing (where permitted), and customer support. These providers are bound by contractual obligations to handle your data securely and only for the purposes specified by us.

6.4 For Legal and Safety Reasons

We may disclose information when we believe in good faith that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request,
  • Enforce our Terms of Use and other policies,
  • Protect the rights, property, and safety of Find Safe, our users, delivery partners, or the public,
  • Detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues.

6.5 Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, sale of assets, or similar transaction, your information may be transferred as part of the business assets. In such a case, we will ensure the receiving party respects this Privacy Policy or provides a similar level of protection.

7. User-Generated Content and Moderation

The Services allow users to create and share content, including listings, photos, descriptions, and messages. We may review or monitor such content, using automated tools or manual review, to detect fraud, scams, prohibited items, and violations of our policies. Content that violates our policies may be removed, and accounts may be restricted or closed.

8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Examples include encryption in transit, access controls, and secure storage systems. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including:

  • For the duration of your account being active,
  • For as long as required to complete transactions and resolve disputes,
  • For as long as required by legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory obligations,
  • For legitimate business needs such as fraud prevention and security.

When your information is no longer needed, we will delete it or anonymize it so that it can no longer be linked to you. If you request deletion of your account, we will deactivate or delete your profile and remove or anonymize personal information, subject to any legal or legitimate retention requirements.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your country or region, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access and obtain a copy of your personal information.
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • The right to data portability, where technically feasible.
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent (for example, for marketing or precise location).

To exercise these rights, you may use the settings in the App where available, or contact us using the details provided in the “Contact Us” section below. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

11. International Data Transfers

We may process and store your information in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards to protect your information during such transfers, such as contractual protections or other lawful mechanisms.

12. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction to consent to data processing). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
