Find Safe – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Find Safe (“Find Safe”, “we”, “our”, “us”). We operate a scam-free marketplace for buying and selling second-hand products and store-listed goods. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all users worldwide who use the Find Safe app and services.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Name, username, email address, phone number
  • Profile photo and account details
  • Addresses (pickup, delivery, billing)
  • In-app chat, feedback, reports, reviews
  • Listing data: item images, descriptions, prices, categories

3.2 Government ID & Address Verification

To maintain a scam-free marketplace, we may collect and verify:

  • Government ID documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving Licence, etc.)
  • Address proof (utility bills, official documents)
  • Selfie photos or short verification videos

This information is strictly used for identity verification, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, and regulatory compliance. It is not shared publicly.

3.3 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by third-party payment processors. We may receive payment status information but do not store full card numbers.

3.4 Location Information

Used for nearby listings, delivery, and fraud detection. You can disable location in your phone settings.

3.5 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information: model, OS, IP, device identifiers
  • Usage data: screens visited, searches, interactions
  • Crash logs and performance analytics

3.6 Cookies, SDKs & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and mobile SDKs to:

  • Enable core app features
  • Analyze performance and improve user experience
  • Serve and optimize advertisements (if enabled)

4. Advertising & Third-Party Ad Networks

Find Safe may display ads to support free usage of the platform. We may integrate advertising services such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • AppLovin MAX
  • Meta Audience Network
  • Unity Ads
  • Other vetted ad partners

4.1 Data Used for Ads

Advertising partners may collect or receive:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location
  • App interactions (e.g., pages visited)
  • Non-sensitive device information

We do NOT share: Government ID, phone numbers, payment information, or chat content with advertising networks.

4.2 Personalized Ads & Opt-Out

Depending on your region, you may receive:

  • Personalized ads (based on your device identifiers)
  • Non-personalized ads (less targeted)

You can disable ad personalization anytime through:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → “Opt Out of Ads Personalization”.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and improve the marketplace
  • Verify user identity to prevent scams
  • Facilitate buying, selling, pickup, and delivery
  • Process payments and commissions
  • Communicate with users about transactions
  • Detect fraud and suspicious behavior
  • Provide customer support
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. How We Share Your Information

6.1 With Other Users

Only basic profile details and listings are shown to others. Never your ID documents, full address, or phone (unless you choose to share phone yourself).

6.2 With Delivery Partners

  • Name and phone (for communication)
  • Pickup/delivery address
  • Order details

6.3 With Service Providers

Such as cloud hosting, payment processors, analytics partners, ad networks, KYC/verification providers.

6.4 For Legal or Safety Reasons

We may disclose information to law enforcement if required.

6.5 Business Transfers

If Find Safe is acquired, user data may transfer as part of the transaction.

7. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, limited-access controls, and other safeguards.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as necessary for fraud prevention, legal compliance, transactions, and safety.

9. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data portability

10. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or local equivalent age).

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in India or other countries where our partners operate.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when required. The latest version will always be published here.
